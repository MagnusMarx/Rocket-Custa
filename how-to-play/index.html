How To Play

GAMEPLAY
The purpose of the game is to avoid the incoming comets for as long as possible. When you collapse with a comet it is game over! You can submit your highscore and/or play the game again in order to improve yourself.

BROWSER & CHROME STORE
You can play with the following controls:
- WASD keys
- Arrow Keys
